Welcome

With B is a roleplay game you can play with your friends or team mates.

You are in a couple with 2 people. 💑💑
They also are in a couple with you and another person.
The goal is to find whom they are cheating on you with ! 🕵️‍♀️🕵️‍♂️
Each player is given 5 random personality traits with at least one in common with both people they are in a couple with.

When you think you figured out with whom is one of your partners cheating on you, you can try to take a guess and hit the buzz button.
Be careful you only get 3 attempts ! 🔥🔥🔥

The main goal is to talk to each other, but keep an eye on your profile : events could happen to spice things up. 😏
